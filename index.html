What is the sharpe ratio calculator?
The sharpe ratio calculator is a tool that determines the amount of potential gains from a risky investment. How to calculate sharpe ratio?
To calculate sharpe ratio you should use the following formula:
sharpe ratio = ((return of the asset or investment / 100) - (risk free return / 100)) / (standard deviation / 100)
